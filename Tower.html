The Tower

Myles Standish Hall, affectionately known as "The Tower," stands as a timeless icon on the Boston University campus. Nestled in the picturesque West Campus, The Tower offers an unparalleled experience for those who seek historic charm, modern comforts, and stunning views of the city.

Room Options

Here are the various room options, to find the ideal setup for your needs.

Single Room

1. Electricity: Each room is provided with electricity to power lighting, devices, and other electrical appliances.
2. Heating and Cooling: Centralized heating and cooling systems ensure a comfortable room temperature throughout the year.
3. Internet Access: High-speed internet connectivity is usually included to support academic and personal online activities.
4. Water: Potable water for drinking, washing, and other daily needs.
5. Trash and Recycling: Collection services for disposing of waste and recyclables within the residence hall.

Double Room

1. Two beds and Mattresses: The room will be furnished with two single beds, each with a comfortable mattress, to accommodate two residents.
2. Desk and Chair: There will be two desks and chairs, providing separate study areas for each resident.
3. Closet or Wardrobe: Ample storage space with two closets or wardrobes to store clothing and personal items.
4. Internet Access: High-speed internet connectivity is available, allowing both residents to stay connected for academic and personal purposes.
5. Heating and Cooling: Climate control is in place to ensure a comfortable room temperature for both residents, with adjustable settings.
