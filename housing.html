List of Dormitories - Your On-Campus Home Options

Below, you'll find a curated list of our featured dormitories, each with its own charm and advantages. Whether you're looking for a bustling hub of campus life or a quieter retreat for focused studying, our list covers a range of housing options to suit every Terrier's needs.
Discover the possibilities, explore the details, and hear what fellow students have to say about their experiences in each dormitory. Your journey to finding the perfect on-campus living space starts here.

Warren Towers

Located at the heart of central campus, Warren Towers offers a vibrant community and easy access to classes, making it a bustling hub for BU life.

The Tower

Myles Standish Hall, affectionately known as 'The Tower' in the picturesque West Campus, provides stunning views and a historic atmosphere for an iconic BU experience.

Claflin Hall

Nestled in the serene East Campus, Claflin Hall offers a cozy atmosphere and proximity to essential academic buildings, creating a welcoming and convenient living environment.

Sleeper Hall

Sleeper Hall in the heart of Central Campus is a peaceful retreat for dedicated students, providing a serene environment to focus on studies and recharge.

Rich Hall

Rich Hall in the vibrant West Campus stands as a modern and comfortable choice, offering contemporary amenities for a well-rounded on-campus experience.
