Claflin Hall

Nestled in the serene East Campus of Boston University, Claflin Hall offers a cozy and convenient living environment for students seeking proximity to essential academic buildings and a supportive community. Explore the unique features and warm ambiance of Claflin Hall, your potential home away from home.

Room Options

Here are the various room options, to find the ideal setup for your needs.

Single Room

1. Bed and Mattress: Each room typically includes a comfortable single bed with a mattress, ensuring a good night's sleep.
2. Desk and Chair: A designated study area with a desk and chair is provided, offering a suitable environment for academic work.
3. Closet or Wardrobe: Ample storage space for clothing and personal belongings, helping you stay organized.
4. Internet Access: High-speed internet connectivity is available for academic and personal use, ensuring you stay connected.
5. Heating and Cooling: Climate control is in place to maintain a comfortable room temperature, allowing you to adjust it to your preference.

Double Room

1. Two beds and Mattresses: The room will be furnished with two single beds, each with a comfortable mattress, to accommodate two residents.
2. Desk and Chair: There will be two desks and chairs, providing separate study areas for each resident.
3. Closet or Wardrobe: Only one closet to store clothing and personal items.
4. Internet Access: High-speed internet connectivity is available, allowing both residents to stay connected for academic and personal purposes.
5. Heating and Cooling: There is no heating.

Triple Room

1. Bunk Beds or Lofted Beds: To maximize space, triple rooms may feature bunk beds or lofted beds, allowing for more floor space.
2. Study Nooks: One available.
3. Modular Storage Solutions: To optimize storage, consider modular storage solutions like under-bed storage drawers or shelving units for each resident.
4. Enhanced Connectivity: Triple rooms may offer upgraded internet connectivity to accommodate three users simultaneously, ensuring smooth online activities.
5. Zoned Climate Control: For personalized comfort, zoned climate control may be available, allowing each resident to adjust their room's temperature to their preference.

Quad Room

1. Bed and Mattress: The room will usually be equipped with four single beds, each furnished with a comfortable mattress to cater to all four residents.
2. Desk and Chair: Four separate desks and chairs will be provided, ensuring ample workspace for individual study needs.
3. Closet or Wardrobe: Ample storage space will be available with four closets or wardrobes, allowing each resident to store their clothing and personal items.
4. Internet Access: High-speed internet connectivity is typically provided to support the online needs of all four residents, whether for academic or personal use.
5. Heating and Cooling: The room will have climate control options to maintain a comfortable room temperature, with settings that can be adjusted to accommodate all residents' preferences.
